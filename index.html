How to Use Study-Link

Welcome to the journey of collaborative learning! Joining a study group is simple and beneficial. Follow these steps to find your team:

  • Browse Groups: Explore the list of available study groups on our 'Join a Group' page.
  • Select a Group: Find a group that matches your course and study interests.
  • Request to Join: Send a request to join the group. Some groups may require a quick introduction.
  • Start Collaborating: Once accepted, you’ll gain access to group chats, study materials, and meeting schedules.
